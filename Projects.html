Previous Research Projects

  • Biomaterials evaluation

    The morphology and actin cytoskeletons of hBMSCs were observed via electron and confocal microscopy, and the cell viability, proliferation and osteogenic differentiation potential of hBMSCs (human Bone Marrow Stem Cells) were examined quantitatively by QPCR, PrestoBlue and Ki67 immunofluorescence. The repair of femur were evaluated by Micro-CT and histomorphology of hard tissue section.

  • Tumor Microenvironment

    With transwell co-culture system, mRNA expression of hBMSCs in Osteosarcoma/hBMSC microenvironment was measured by microarray. The signaling pathways with significant changes were found by GSEA/David and verified by QPCR, proliferation, migration and invasion assays.
